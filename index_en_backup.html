About Us

Shenzhen Cosy International Logistics Co., Ltd.

Cosy International Logistics is an integrated international logistics service provider, headquartered in Shenzhen, China (a key logistics hub). With our "Cosy" brand philosophy, we deliver convenient, secure, and efficient logistics experiences for global clients.

Mission: Simplify logistics processes through technology and enhance cross-border trade with trusted services.

Vision: Become a world-leading "cosy-style" international logistics service provider.
