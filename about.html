About Cosy International Logistics 关于舒室国际物流

公司简介

Company Overview

舒室国际物流有限公司成立于2010年，总部位于中国上海，是一家专业的国际物流服务提供商。我们致力于为全球客户提供高效、可靠的综合物流解决方案。

Founded in 2010 and headquartered in Shanghai, China, Cosy International Logistics Co., Ltd. is a professional international logistics service provider. We are committed to providing efficient and reliable comprehensive logistics solutions to global clients.

我们的使命

Our Mission

通过创新的物流技术和专业的服务团队，为客户创造最大价值，成为全球领先的综合物流解决方案提供商。

Through innovative logistics technology and professional service teams, we create maximum value for our clients and become a globally leading comprehensive logistics solutions provider.

我们的愿景

Our Vision

构建全球智能物流网络，让世界贸易更加便捷高效。

Building a global intelligent logistics network to make world trade more convenient and efficient.

全球网络

Global Network

舒室国际物流在全球拥有超过50个分支机构和办事处，服务网络覆盖亚洲、欧洲、北美、南美、非洲和大洋洲等100多个国家和地区。我们在主要港口城市和经济中心都设有专业团队，确保为客户提供本地化的优质服务。

Cosy International Logistics has over 50 branches and offices worldwide, with a service network covering more than 100 countries and regions across Asia, Europe, North America, South America, Africa, and Oceania. We have professional teams in major port cities and economic centers to ensure high-quality localized services for our clients.

专业团队

Professional Team

我们的团队由超过500名经验丰富的物流专业人士组成，包括国际货运专家、供应链管理顾问、技术支持工程师和客户服务专员。团队成员平均拥有8年以上的行业经验，能够为客户提供全方位的专业支持。

Our team consists of over 500 experienced logistics professionals, including international freight experts, supply chain management consultants, technical support engineers, and customer service specialists. Our team members have an average of over 8 years of industry experience, providing comprehensive professional support to our clients.
